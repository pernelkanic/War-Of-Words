What ?

War of Words (WoW) is the inter-collegiate annual literary fest of Rajalakshmi Engineering College organized jointly by REC Atrium, the literary club of the college, and REC Debating Union, the debating society of the college. WoW’24, an extravaganza that celebrates English literature and creativity, will provide a platform for students across disciplines to exhibit their oratory skills, debating flair, and literary prowess through numerous events. The event WoW’24 is planned to be conducted on 26th February 2024.

Where ?

Rajalakshmi Engineering College, Rajalakshmi Nagar, Thandalam, Chennai - 602 105.

When ?

Monday, 26th February, 2024

WoW Debate

The court is now in session.

WoW Debate allows teams to take part in a Parliamentary debate and use their wit and persuasiveness to battle it out and convince the judges that they are right.

Rules:

1. Teams should have four to five people.

2. Subjects are presented immediately, with 20 minutes allotted for preparation.

3. Each team must make a four-minute pitch live on stage.

4. Should competitors go over the allotted time, judges reserve the right to disqualify the team.

5. Teams can select from a variety of creative expression methods, such as role playing, brief skits, jingles, short speeches, or any other creative format.

6. Teams are permitted to bring the necessary properties.
